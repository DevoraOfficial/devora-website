Built for Privacy-Conscious Users

Devora Vault is a secure, offline password manager designed for users who value their privacy above all else.

In a world where data breaches are common and cloud services are constantly targeted, Devora Vault takes a different approach. All your sensitive information — passwords, notes, credit card details — stays on your device. No servers. No cloud. No third parties.

The application uses AES-256-GCM encryption combined with PBKDF2-SHA256 key derivation to ensure your vault is practically impossible to crack. Your master password is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

Completely free and open-source
No tracking or analytics
Export/import in standard formats
Automatic lock after inactivity

Technical Specifications

  • Encryption: AES-256-GCM
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2-SHA256
  • Storage: Local SQLite database
  • File Format: .vault (encrypted container)
  • Password Generator: Customizable length & character sets
  • Backup: Manual export to encrypted JSON
